About Us

The RRF January 2025 Batch is an online course focusing on web development fundamentals, including HTML, CSS, basic JavaScript, basic PHP, and WordPress. The course comprises 30 classes, each lasting one hour, aiming to equip students with essential skills in web development. Classes are conducted online by Rasel Ahmed, providing flexibility for students to participate from any location. Live sessions are streamed on his Facebook page, and recordings are available on YouTube for review. This course offers an excellent opportunity for beginners interested in web development to acquire necessary skills and knowledge.
